hot or not

Making it easier to talk about the weather.

This app came from meeting a bunch of people whose brains work in Celsius — and realizing mine doesn't.

I didn't just want to memorize a formula. I wanted to actually feel what 22°C means, the way I already know what 72°F feels like. The only way to build that intuition is repetition — so I built this.

One temperature at a time.
